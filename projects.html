PROJECTS

 

GLOBAL C is a Multi-disciplinary Consulting & Advisory Firm that provides integrated Project Management, Comprehensive Architecture, Structure & MEP Engineering Services for Project Developers, Lenders and Users. Our participation in development of esteemed Infrastructure, Interiors & Real Estate Projects of the recent times is our Reputation we have to live up to. We have gathered a huge knowledge base over the years which provide credibility to our experience and ensure our Delivery lives up to our commitment. Providing strategic inputs and executing agreed delivery methodology during all phases of the development cycle - from conceptualization to final commissioning is a key approach our project delivery team follows.

We proactively classify and control the risks inherent in the preconstruction stage, planning, design and construction of different types of projects including land development, new construction and rehabilitation, adaptive re-use renovation and expansion.
